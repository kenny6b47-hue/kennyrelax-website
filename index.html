About

Kenny Relax

Kenny Relax is a South Floridian by way of New York artist and songwriter carving his own lane with a distinctive sound rooted in trap — raw, authentic, and unapologetic. From his breakout records to his latest album MR. RELAX, he brings a rare blend of lyrical depth and street energy that resonates worldwide.
